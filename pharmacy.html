About Us
Ghandour Pharamcy was Esablished in 2020

It is a retail store that provides medicines, drugs, pharmaceuticals, and other products. The pharmacist supervises the fulfillment of prescriptions and is available to provide advice on the medications to be given. Pharmacists dispense medicines as recommended by doctors and prescriptions given, and also dispense other medicines (shelf medicines) that do not need a prescription; The pharmacist will be able to provide general information about medicines, vitamins, or the way to use auxiliary materials, such as patches, bandages, inhalers, many healthy herbs, and others. A pharmacy is a healthcare facility that emphasizes the provision of pharmaceutical services to a specific community. It also dispenses the medicine and includes the presence of a registered registered pharmacist. The pharmacy could be affiliated with the private sector, or it could be a public institution affiliated with the Ministry of Health in which the processes of formulating, dispensing, reviewing and ensuring their efficiency are carried out. Modern tasks such as patient care known as clinical pharmacology, such as measuring blood pressure, or, in some European countries, also appeared, blood analysis and cholesterol determination during certain weeks of the year. There are several types of pharmacies, the most famous of which are general, hospital pharmacies, military pharmacies, veterinary pharmacies, and others. .
